DESCRIPTION

This competition tests your ability to capture beauty, emotion, and storytelling through photography. Participants must demonstrate strong composition, lighting control, and originality while transforming ordinary moments into meaningful visual narratives.

RULES & GUIDELINES

1) Event rounds and selection procedures may be adjusted by the event authorities.

2) For the first round, participants must submit photographs under the theme "Lights and Shadows, Silence and Story".

3) Files must be submitted only in JPEG, JPG, or HEIC format.

4) All images must include a clearly visible watermark (DATE AND TIME).

5) Each photograph must follow photography principles such as rule of thirds or symmetrical composition with clear subject focus and balanced lighting.

6) No editing, filters, retouching, or AI-generated content is allowed.

7) Photographs must retain natural color tones and lighting.

8) Submitted photographs must be original works owned by the participant.

9) Use of stock images or internet images is strictly prohibited.

10) Each participant must submit exactly four photographs in the following ratios: 9:16, 3:4, and 1:1.

11) Group submissions or collaborative entries are not allowed.

12) Any violation of rules will result in disqualification.

13) Judges’ decisions are final and binding.
