DESCRIPTION

The Short Film Event provides a platform for students to showcase original short films with an open theme. Explore any genre and present your vision through impactful storytelling.

UNLEASH YOUR CREATIVITY AND TELL POWERFUL STORIES THROUGH CINEMA!

RULES & GUIDELINES

1)A team can have a maximum of 5 presenting members.

2)Duration: 10–15 minutes

3) Language: Tamil or English

4) Films must be original.

5) Content should be decent and respectful; vulgar or inappropriate content is strictly prohibited.

6) Video format: MP4 / MOV (Minimum 720p).

7) Films must be submitted on or before the given deadline.

8) Late or incomplete submissions will not be accepted

9) The decision of the judges will be final.

10) The final movie must be sent to AAVISHKAR’S official mail ID, and participants must carry the movie in a pen drive on the event day.
