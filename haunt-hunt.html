DESCRIPTION

Haunt and Hunt is a thrilling solo challenge where participants race against time to complete a series of spooky, fun, and brain-teasing tasks. From quick physical activities to sharp observation tests, each clue must be solved in sequence without any external help. The fastest participant to crack all clues wins, with certificates for both winners and participants

RULES & GUIDELINES

1) This is a solo event — only one participant per entry.

2) The event will involve quick 1–2 minute tasks including spooky, physical, and observation challenges.

3) Use of mobile phones or external help is strictly prohibited.

4) Damaging props or tampering with other participants’ clues will lead to immediate disqualification.

5) The first participant to find the surprise will be declared the winner.

6) Certificates will be awarded to both winners and all participants.
