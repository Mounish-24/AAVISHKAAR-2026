DESCRIPTION

The Free Fire Tournament is a competitive E-Sports event designed to bring together skilled players to battle for supremacy in intense survival-based matches.

This tournament provides a fair and exciting platform for participants to showcase their reflexes, strategic gameplay, teamwork, and combat skills.

The event aims to promote E-Sports culture, encourage healthy competition, and identify top Free Fire talents through structured and transparent gameplay.

RULES & GUIDELINES

1) Round 1: Qualifying round – Three matches will be conducted and points will be considered.

2) Round 2: Final Clash Squad match will be conducted and winner will be chosen.

3) Fair Play: No hacks, bugs, character attributes, or gun attributes are allowed.

4) Teaming: In-game team forming is strictly prohibited. Violators will be eliminated.

5) Match Type: Only Squad matches are allowed (3–4 members per team).

6) Restrictions: Solo and Duo matches are not permitted.
