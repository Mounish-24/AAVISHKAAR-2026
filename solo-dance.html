DESCRIPTION

Step into the spotlight and let your talent shine! Solo Dance is a platform for individual dancers to express their passion, creativity, and confidence through movement. Participants will mesmerize the audience with their expressions, rhythm, and stage presence.

1) Participation: This is a Solo Dance event; only one participant is allowed per performance.

6) Conduct: Obscene gestures, inappropriate costumes, offensive lyrics, or any indecent or violent behavior will lead to immediate disqualification.

7) Time Violation: Exceeding the time limit will result in point deductions.

8) Decision: Judges’ decision is final and binding.

9) Certificates: Certificates will be awarded to both winners and all participants.
